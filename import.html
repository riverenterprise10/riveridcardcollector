📂 Step 1: Upload Excel/CSV Document

Select an Excel (`.xlsx`, `.xls`) or Comma Separated Values (`.csv`) spreadsheet file. The app will parse each row as a data record, automatically assign consecutive photo IDs (e.g. `001`, `002`), and let you map the columns to your dynamic fields.

💡 Pro Tip for Importing:

  • ✔ Column headers in your Excel sheet will automatically map to your active settings fields.
  • ✔ Any new columns found in the sheet can be auto-created as custom fields automatically!
  • ✔ Records are created in the draft queue instantly so you can upload pictures at your own pace.
